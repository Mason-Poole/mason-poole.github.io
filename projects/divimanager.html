DiviManager

In September of 2023, I began working as a contracted software developer/IT manager for Shepherds Loft, a local web development and hosting company. My main position was as a developer for DiviManager, a proprietary WordPress plugin used to manage other WordPress sites from one interface, removing the need to access each site individually.

The main features I helped implement include remote WordPress core updates, remote site creation, remote plugin and theme deletion, profile-based plugin limits, and bulk plugin/theme updates. These features have reduced the time and cost of routine maintenance, expedited the client onboarding process, and provided important information that influenced business decisions. Additionally, I did a significant amount of debugging, using both manual and automated testing to identify issues in the program, which were then logged in Trello and GitHub before being resolved.

My time working at Shepherds Loft has been one of immense growth, giving me a plethora of hands-on experience working in a company environment. I worked directly with other developers and the business owner to design, document, and implement features that directly impacted the company and its clients. DiviManager is the largest project I've worked on so far, and I was successfully able to come into a project several years in the making, navigate and understand the existing code and structure, and expand upon it while maintaining the company's coding standards. My time at Shepherds Loft has largely shaped me as a professional developer and will forever remain a crucial stage in my career journey.
